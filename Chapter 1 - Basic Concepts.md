## 1.2.1 Mathematical Induction

Upper bound of Fibonacci: 0, 1, 1, 2... (starting from $F_0$)
$$
  F_n \le \phi^{n-1}
$$
Where $\phi=(1+\sqrt{5})/2$, notice $\phi^2=1+\phi$.
